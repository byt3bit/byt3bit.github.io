I am a lecturer at the Alpen-Adria-University Klagenfurt. 

Prior to this I was a senior research associate at the University of Bristol .

## Research

The topics of my research interests are

* Cryptanalysis and design of symmetric-key algorithms
* Symmetric-key cryptography for Zero-knowledge and MPC applications
* Side-channel cryptanalysis and efficient countermeasures
* Quantum algorithms for cryptanalysis of symmetric-key functions

I am co-designer of the block cipher and hash function families _MiMC_ and _GMiMC_ and have been actively involved in their design, analysis and implementation. These two families are constructed to have _low multiplicative complexity_ over large fields. More about such constructions, their applications and updates about new design can be found [here](https://byt3bit.github.io/primesym/).

Recently, I have been working on the design and analysis of _forkcipher_. I am co-designer of the forkcipher called _ForkSkinny_. Forkcipher is particularly designed for a new lightweight Authenticated Encryption (AE) of very short messages called ForkAE. This new design is now one of the [Round 2 candidates](https://csrc.nist.gov/Projects/lightweight-cryptography/round-2-candidates) in the standardization process of lightweight AE, organized by NIST.

More information about these works and my publications can be found [here](https://byt3bit.github.io/publication/).
